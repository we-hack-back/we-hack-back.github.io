How we work

Built on security principles you can verify

Every case follows documented playbooks, transparent communication, and industry standards designed to keep your data safe.

Vetted responders

Incident handlers with enterprise security backgrounds and public-interest experience, backed by NDAs for sensitive work.

Documented process

Clear runbooks for triage, containment, and recovery mapped to NIST CSF so you always know the next step.

Data handling discipline

Scoped access, encrypted handoffs, and minimal data retention to protect evidence and privacy.

Victim-centered approach

Communication that avoids blame, prioritizes your consent, and keeps you in control of decisions.

  • 4-hour response goal for new cases and a dedicated point of contact throughout the incident.
  • Post-incident hardening plan with prioritized fixes and training materials tailored to your risk.
  • Partnership-friendly: ready to collaborate with your legal counsel, insurer, or MSP.
  • Respectful of your pace: we align with what feels safe for you and your household or team.
